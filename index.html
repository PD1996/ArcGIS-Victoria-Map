ArcGIS Victoria Map

This map displays voting locations within Victoria, BC. It also displays vehicle traffic density and available bike routes within the city. The aim of the map is to reduce vehicle traffic by showing people where they can bike to when it comes time to vote. You can also learn the type, electoral division, building name and address of each voting location by clicking them on the map.
